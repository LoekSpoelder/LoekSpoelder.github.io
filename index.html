Cybersecurity

One of the big projects I did was a hacking challenge to get root access to a given IP-adress, that’s it, no additional information was given. Eventually me and my partner managed to do it the fastest out of the 3 groups attempting it.

Quantumcomputing

Another big project I did was participating in the Quantum Quest: a five week course hosted by a handful of universities in Europe every other year going into the basics and not-so-basics of quantum computing.

FPVRC-Car

This was my final school project, a first person view remote controlled car. It started off pretty strong but in the end we faced too many obstacles to meet the deadline so we never got a finished product. It ended up teaching me a lot about personal projects, self-study, C++, embedded systems and RC-cars.
